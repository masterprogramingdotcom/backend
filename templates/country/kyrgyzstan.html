About Kyrgyzstan

Below are some noticeable things about Kyrgyzstan:

Kyrgyzstan (officially the Kyrgyz Republic), known as the "Switzerland of Central Asia" is a mountainous nation with un believably stunning terrains. With more than half of the popu lation under the age of 25, Kyrgyzstan, at the age of 30, is a youthful country. Their official languages are Kyrgyz and Kyrgyzstan. Kyrgyzstan declared its independence on August 31, 1991 and named Bishkek Its new capital. It is considered one of the most beau tiful as well as safer countries in Central Asia.

The International High School of Medicine,

founded in 1993, in the pinnacle of medicine education. IHSM has the older and features one of the most magnificent structure in Bishkek. This institution was founded with the goal of providing the finest quality education and implementing a comprehensive education reform approach. The university offers a 5.5year medical program. It has 3 ex quisite campuses, 5 IHSM clinics, and 2 morphological centers. The university has 10 percent academic mobility and around 1600 alumni members of IHSM are working worldwide.

International High School of Medicine (Issyk-Kul)

is the International School Of Medicine structural division locat ed in the town of Cholpon-Ata on a shore of Issyk-Kul lake. The university functions close to the International School Of Medi cine campus in Bishkek and provides training of 5 years of MD Degree in General Medicine. The administrative subdivisions and departments located in the Issyk-Kul campus also tightly coordinate their activities with the International School Of Medicine departments located at the Central Campus. The campus's training units are equipped with all the necessary study materials to ensure active learning.

KYRGYZ STATE MEDICAL ACADEMY (KSMA)

Is one the best oldest universities, located in Bishkek. The university has a modern infrastructure and offers low-cost, high-quality medical education. A total number of 15,000 students are currently study ing and among them, 20 percent of international stu dents. The faculties at Kyrgyz state medical academy are well qualified and some of them have received state award winners. The professors are supportive and are available to help students even beyond collage working hours.

Admission Process

The admission procedure at the Kazan State Medical University is as follows:

A prospective student can apply online as instructed below:-

Step 1: Open the application form.

Step 2: Fill in the application form accurately.

Step 3: Attach the documents given below

  • Scanned copy of marks (grades) of completion of higher secondary education / tertiary education (should be translated into Kyrgyzstan language)
  • Results of NEET (only for candidates from India)
  • Scanned copy of your Passport (should be translated into Kyrgyzstan language) Students should send all the documents on help@ismoedu.in

Step 4: After fulfilling all the conditions and upon acceptance students will be provided with a provisional admission letter (in electronic format) upon request.

Step 5: As soon as your visa invitation will be ready, the university will mail you your visa invitation.

Step 6: Stamp your visa in the Embassy (Consulate) of Kyrgyzstan Federation, once you receive the visa invitation.
