About Kazakhstan

Below are some noticeable things about Kazakhstan:

Kazakhstan, located in the center of Eurasia, first came on the global map in 1991. The word Kazakh, which is of Turkic origin, is supposed to signify "free man" which is fitting for a nation historically populated by free-spirited nomsds. Kazkhstan's economic potential is immense. The diverse terrain runs from the mountainous, densely populated eastern regions to the thinly inhabited, abundantly energy-rich low lands of the west, as well as from the industrialized north, with its Siberian ice cap.

Kokshetau State University (KSU)

Founded in 1962. Sh. Ualikhanov Kokshetau State University (KSU) is a modern educational institution in Kokshetau, Kazakhstan. The university has a 35th country rank and an approx number of 8000 students have enrolled. The in stitution provides practical and theoretical knowledge at a low cost and has almost 30 university collaborations. The university has 9 teaching buildings and offers 107 academic programs. The university has received NAAR accreditation for 50+ educational programs that provide high-quality medical education.

The Kazakh Russian Medical University (KRMU)

The Kazakh Russian Medical University (KRMU) is one of the country's largest private universities, with four official certificates. It is originally the Kazakhstani Medical Institute, first opened its doors in 1992, following substantial political and economic reforms in which science and higher education reforms were given a prominent role. Dr. Mukhtar Aliyevich Aliyev, a member of the Republic of Kazakhstan Academy of Sciences, founded KRMU alongside professor K.M. Maskeev and assistant professors V.S. Holubchenko and A.O. Panova. The university's educational curriculum meets national standards, and more than 5000 students have enrolled in the MBBS course.

Caspian international school of medicine

Caspian international school of medicine, is a multidisciplinary institution that provides medical students with high-quality, comprehensive training. The institution has around 200 professors, and students get medical training at renowned clinical locations in Almaty. Caspian international school of medicine has associations with universities both in the country and abroad. As a result, the academic mobility program provides students with the chance to practice and study abroad.

Admission Process

The admission procedure at the Kazan State Medical University is as follows:

A prospective student can apply online as instructed below:-

Step 1: Open the application form.

Step 2: Fill in the application form accurately.

Step 3: Attach the documents given below

  • Scanned copy of marks (grades) of completion of higher secondary education / tertiary education (should be translated into Kazakhstan language)
  • Results of NEET (only for candidates from India)
  • Scanned copy of your Passport (should be translated into Kazakhstan language) Students should send all the documents on help@ismoedu.in

Step 4: After fulfilling all the conditions and upon acceptance students will be provided with a provisional admission letter (in electronic format) upon request.

Step 5: As soon as your visa invitation will be ready, the university will mail you your visa invitation.

Step 6: Stamp your visa in the Embassy (Consulate) of Kazakhstan Federation, once you receive the visa invitation.
