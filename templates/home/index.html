About ISMO

ISMO Educational consultant is one of the fast growing consultancy with dynamic management which aim to provide medical education for students who has an aspiration to become a doctor. We provide best support in various stages of foreign education process and provide a safe Indian environment with all aminities required to study and learn that helps aspirants to excel in their career. We help you get budget-friendly universities based on your profile. Individual attention to each aspi rant through our 1:1 sessions. This support is provided throughout the whole process.
ISM Edutech was created in 2010 with the goal of scaling up excellent healthcare services and attaining greater heights in the future. It is the leading company providing international medical education services. It has come a long way in the last 12 years and has made a significant contribution. Begun with a few students, this company has sent over 9,000 students to the world's greatest med ical universities in Tajikistan, Kyrgyzstan, Kazakhstan Georgia and Barbados.
